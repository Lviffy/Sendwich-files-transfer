About Us

Welcome to Sendwich, your reliable file transfer solution. We are dedicated to providing you with the best service to send and receive files quickly and securely.

Our mission is to make file sharing as easy and efficient as possible. Whether you are sending large files to colleagues or sharing memories with friends, Sendwich is here to help.

Sendwich was built in a single night by two dedicated students. We are not a large organization, but our passion for technology drives us to continuously improve our platform.

Thank you for choosing Sendwich. We look forward to serving you!
